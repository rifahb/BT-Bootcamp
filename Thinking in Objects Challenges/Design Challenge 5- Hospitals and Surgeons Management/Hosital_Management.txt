1. Problem Statement (Summary)

A surgeon management system where:

A surgeon works for many hospitals

A surgeon conducts operations on patients

Patients are admitted to wards

Surgeons are of different types (Senior, Non-Senior)

The system should:

Find total number of patients being operated

List all patients being operated by a surgeon

List all patients admitted to a ward

2️. Identified Classes

From the problem statement:

Surgeon (base class)

SeniorSurgeon

NonSeniorSurgeon

Hospital

Ward

Patient

Operation

3️. Relationships (OO Design)

Surgeon works for many Hospitals

Surgeon performs many Operations

Operation involves one Patient

Ward admits many Patients

SeniorSurgeon / NonSeniorSurgeon IS-A Surgeon
