1. Problem Statement (Summary)

A marine company owns multiple ships.
Ships can be of two types:

Cruise ships (for passenger travel)

Cargo ships (for transporting goods)

Customers register/book a ship to:

Travel from one place to another (cruise)

Send cargo from one place to another (cargo)

The system should:

Find the total amount collected by the company

Find the total amount collected for each ship

List all customer details for a particular cruise ship

CruiseShip
